XP World Border

A plugin where your survival determines the world size.

XP World Border adds a unique twist to your Minecraft server by making player levels control the size of the world border. As players gain XP, the border grows, giving everyone more room to explore. The plugin also lets players claim land and create special factions where XP boosts your claim size, adding a fun, strategic layer. With cool new items like XP Multiplier Boots and full customizability from a server-owning perspective, you'll have everything you need to keep your server exciting.
